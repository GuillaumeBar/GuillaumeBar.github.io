Here is our selection of the top 3 best restaurants and bars in town:

360 restaurant

360 The Restaurant at the CN Tower, one of Toronto's finest, features Canadian cuisine combined with an outstanding revolving view of Toronto 350 metres below. 360 offers market-fresh cuisine, featuring Canadian ingredients to ensure an incomparable culinary experience.

Go to their website >

Big Smoke Burger

Their burgers feature gourmet ingredients that are sourced from top quality and local producers. Their menu features hand-cut French fries made fresh every day. Hand-spun milkshakes, house-made sauces, traditional poutine and a selection of unique beverages are also available.

Go to their website >

Bar Hop

This is not just another bar. It is a destination that friends tell friends about where they can enjoy high quality beverages with complementing food. With an approachability, an experienced staff, and a relaxed atmosphere, it is a great place to enjoy one of their 36 brews on draught, 100 bottled beers and other spirits, ciders and cocktails.

Go to their website >

Real Sports Bar

At Real Sports Bar & Grill, the top-notch culinary team knows exactly what you want to eat. Forget the fancy. Feel-good favourites are what they are about. They have 10 wing flavours including the signature Thai Sweet Chili award-winner, 5 gourmet burgers, and a whole roster of reinvented crave-worthy classics to satisfy your appetite.

Go to their website >

Bar Chef

Toronto's BarChef, as the name implies, equates the skill and invention of the people behind the bar with the city's top gastronomic innovators. These aren't just bartenders, these are bar-chefs, using fresh ingredients and complex recipes to create drinks you'll remember. BarChef is a stylish venue, with the apothecary-like bar very much the focus of the room.

Go to their website >
